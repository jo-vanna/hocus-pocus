The Concept

Where Complexity Meets Clarity

The phrase "hocus pocus" originally mocked fake magic, nonsense words used by charlatans to distract while they pulled tricks. Sound familiar?

We're calling out the expensive fiction: AI hype that evaporates on implementation, consultants who complicate simple things, transformation theatre with big words and questionable outcomes.

This is the anti-magic show. Real conversation. Practical insights. No smoke machines.

The Format: An offline podcast. Two hosts in conversation, not presentation. You're eavesdropping on something real.

The Room: 20 seats. No recording. No slides. Just the conversation that never happens at conferences.

The Feel: A private salon meets a late-night podcast. Intellectual but never stuffy. Bring your skepticism.
